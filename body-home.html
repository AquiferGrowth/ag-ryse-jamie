Why I Started This Blog

I got tired of buying tech that looked great in marketing videos but fell flat in real life. So I started testing products myself, documenting what actually worked, and sharing honest takes with people who value authenticity over hype.

Living in a 650 sq ft NYC apartment means every purchase needs to earn its place. No room for gadgets that don't deliver. That constraint became my superpower — I only recommend what survives real-world use.

This blog is for people who want real insights, not affiliate-driven recommendations. If you're optimizing your space or upgrading your routine, you're in the right place.
